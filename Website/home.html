About Us

Welcome to STEM Astronomy Club – where the universe comes alive! In December 2017, we founded our club which is dedicated to sharing the wonders of astronomy with enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're gazing up at the night sky for the first time or you're a seasoned stargazer, we’re here to make the cosmos more accessible, fascinating, and fun. Our mission? To spread the love of astronomy far and wide. Join us as we embark on journeys across the cosmos, unlock the secrets of the stars, and cultivate a community that’s as passionate about astronomy as you are!

What We Do

Weekly Cosmic Explorations: Every week, we dive into a new topic – from black holes and supernovae to the science of exoplanets and life beyond Earth. Our discussions are interactive, insightful, and designed to leave you starstruck! House Teams & Friendly Competition: Like the Hogwarts houses, each member joins one of our cosmic houses, building bonds and competing in friendly challenges. You’ll work with your housemates on group activities and share what you learn. Assignments: To sharpen your knowledge, we offer engaging assignments graded by our academic heads, complete with feedback to help you learn and improve Stress-Free Fun: We don’t just stick to academics! We also organize a variety of fun challenges and competitions between houses to keep the atmosphere light and enjoyable. There’s always something happening at STEM Astronomy Club.
